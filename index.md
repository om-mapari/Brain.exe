## Welcome! 👋  

You’ve landed on my little corner of the internet – a living collection of notes, ideas, thoughts straight from my brain.

Think of this space as my digital notebook. Here you’ll find:

- half-formed thoughts
- stuff i like
- some nerdy things
- life
- engineering explorations
- lessons learned
- and random stuff I found interesting

Most of what you see here was originally written **just for myself**. So yes, some notes may feel unfinished, messy, or unpolished. And that’s perfectly okay.

This site is my attempt at learning in public and sharing the journey honestly.

If something looks wrong, outdated, or confusing, feel free to reach out. I genuinely appreciate constructive feedback and new perspectives 🙌
